# Service Recovery

Category: general
Applies to: Complaints, delayed support, poor service experiences, and customer dissatisfaction
Policy: Acknowledge the customer's experience, summarize the issue without being defensive, and provide a concrete next step or ownership path.
Steps:
1. Acknowledge the complaint and the impact on the customer.
2. Confirm the unresolved issue and the expected outcome.
3. Provide the next support step and a clear ownership path.
4. Escalate threats, safety concerns, fraud, or legal claims.
Escalation rules: Escalate complaints that include legal action, police, fraud, account compromise, or immediate safety concerns.
Keywords: service, complaint, delayed support, recovery, apology, response, unacceptable, angry
