# Refund Policy

Category: billing
Applies to: Refund requests, accidental purchases, duplicate charges
Policy: Refund requests are reviewed against order status, delivery state, plan terms, and risk signals. Agents may explain eligibility, but money movement requires human approval before execution.
Steps:
1. Confirm the customer identity and order number.
2. Check whether the order is delivered, shipped, processing, or disputed.
3. Create a refund request for eligible orders and send it to the approval queue.
4. Tell the customer that a specialist will review the request before funds move.
Escalation rules: Escalate if the customer mentions fraud, chargeback, legal action, police, or account takeover.
Keywords: refund, duplicate charge, money back, return, billing, charge, payment
