# Password Reset

Category: account
Applies to: Login issues, forgotten password, reset email not received
Policy: Password reset help can be handled automatically when no account compromise is reported. Account takeover or hacked account language must be escalated.
Steps:
1. Ask the customer to request a fresh reset link from the sign-in page.
2. Ask them to check spam, promotions, and corporate mail filters.
3. Confirm the email address used on the account.
4. Escalate only if the customer reports unauthorized access or a hacked account.
Escalation rules: Escalate for hacked, account takeover, unauthorized login, police, or legal language.
Keywords: password, reset, login, sign in, email link, account
