# Fraud Escalation Policy

Category: fraud
Applies to: Unauthorized charge, hacked account, chargeback, account takeover
Policy: Fraud, legal, police, emergency, and account takeover claims must be handled by a human specialist. Automated tools must not refund, cancel, or change account data on these tickets.
Steps:
1. Mark the ticket urgent and high risk.
2. Stop automated action execution.
3. Preserve trace details and route to the human escalation queue.
4. Send a calm acknowledgement that a specialist will review.
Escalation rules: Always escalate fraud, unauthorized charge, chargeback, hacked account, legal threat, lawsuit, police, or emergency.
Keywords: fraud, unauthorized charge, chargeback, hacked, account takeover, legal, lawsuit, police, emergency
