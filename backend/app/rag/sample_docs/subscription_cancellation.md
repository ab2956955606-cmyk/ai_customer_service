# Subscription Cancellation

Category: account
Applies to: Subscription cancellation, plan changes, downgrade questions
Policy: Customers can cancel future renewals from account settings. Plan downgrades, account deletion, and enterprise contract changes should be reviewed before data or billing changes occur.
Steps:
1. Direct the customer to Account Settings > Billing > Manage plan.
2. Explain that cancellation stops the next renewal but does not remove historical invoices.
3. Offer human help for enterprise, deletion, or downgrade requests.
Escalation rules: Escalate if cancellation is paired with legal threats, account compromise, or data deletion.
Keywords: cancel subscription, downgrade, plan, billing, renewal, account settings
