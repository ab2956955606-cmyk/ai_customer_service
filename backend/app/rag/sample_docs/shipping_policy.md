# Shipping Policy

Category: shipping
Applies to: Late shipments, address updates, delivery questions
Policy: Support can share shipment status and shipping policy details. Address changes after payment are data-changing actions and require approval before execution.
Steps:
1. Locate the order by order number or customer email.
2. Explain the current shipping status.
3. For address changes, create a pending action with the requested new address.
4. For late shipments, provide the delivery window and escalation option.
Escalation rules: Escalate if the shipment issue includes safety, fraud, legal threats, or police reports.
Keywords: shipping, shipment, address, delivery, tracking, late, order, package
