# Invoice Policy

Category: billing
Applies to: Invoice copies, receipts, tax details, billing history
Policy: Customers can download invoices from the billing page. Support may explain invoice access and tax fields. Manual billing corrections or refunds require approval.
Steps:
1. Direct the customer to Billing > Invoices.
2. Tell them to choose the relevant billing period.
3. Explain that VAT or tax IDs can be updated before the next invoice.
4. Create an internal task for manual corrections when needed.
Escalation rules: Escalate if the invoice issue includes fraud, chargeback, legal demand, or unauthorized charge.
Keywords: invoice, receipt, tax, VAT, billing history, payment record
